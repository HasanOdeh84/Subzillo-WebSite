Privacy Policy

This page explains what data Subzillo collects, why we collect it, and the rights you have over your information. We default to collecting the minimum amount of data necessary to provide the service.

Last updated: To be set at launch
Pre-launch placeholder. This document is a working draft and will be reviewed and finalised by counsel before public launch. The summary below reflects the principles Subzillo is being built on.

1. Who we are

Subzillo is an AI-powered subscription management product. When this policy refers to "we", "us" or "Subzillo", it means the company that operates the Subzillo apps, web app and browser extensions.

2. What we collect

To run the service we collect a small set of categories:

  • Account data — your name, email, country and chosen plan.
  • Subscription data you add — the names, prices, renewal dates and categories of the subscriptions you track. You control what goes in.
  • Connected sources (optional) — if you choose to connect Gmail, Outlook or a bank statement scanner, we use those signals only to identify recurring charges.
  • Usage telemetry — basic, anonymised metrics about which features you use so we can prioritise improvements.
What we never collect

Your passwords for third-party services (the AI Browser handles those on-device only), your full browsing history, contact lists, or any data not directly required to manage subscriptions.

3. How we use it

We use the data above to:

  • Show you a clear picture of your subscriptions, spending and renewals.
  • Power Subzi AI's recommendations (e.g. "you haven't used this in 60 days").
  • Let the AI Browser fetch, cancel, upgrade or downgrade subscriptions on your behalf — actions you trigger yourself.
  • Send you product updates, renewal reminders and account notices.
  • Improve reliability, security and performance.

4. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with carefully selected processors needed to run the service — for example, cloud hosting, error reporting and payment processing. Each is bound by contract to handle data on our instructions and to comparable security standards.

5. Where it lives

Account data and subscription records are stored on encrypted infrastructure operated by industry-standard cloud providers. Credentials used by the AI Browser are encrypted on your device and never transmitted to Subzillo servers.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you have rights to access, correct, export or delete the data we hold about you. You can act on most of these directly from your account settings, or by emailing privacy@subzillo.com.

7. Cookies & analytics

The marketing website uses a small number of first-party cookies to remember preferences and measure traffic. We avoid third-party tracking pixels wherever possible. A cookie preferences screen will be available at launch.

8. Children

Subzillo is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a minor has signed up, contact us and we will remove the account.

9. Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we handle data, we'll notify users by email and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after a change means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or about your personal data? Email privacy@subzillo.com or info@subzillo.com.
